## English

> This page does not document personal suffering,  
> offer psychological diagnosis,  
> or provide emotional consolation.  
>  
> It summarizes only challenges  
> that recur across contexts and can be understood structurally.

### 1. Loss of Direction  
Many are not lacking effort,  
but no longer know whether their path is worth continuing.

### 2. Chronic Insecurity  
Even when life is manageable,  
a stable sense of security is absent.

### 3. Loss of Agency  
People can identify problems clearly,  
yet feel incapable of acting without self-destruction.

### 4. Emotional and Cognitive Exhaustion  
This is often prolonged fatigue and numbness,  
not acute distress.

### 5. Erosion of Social Trust  
Trust in rules, institutions, and others declines.

> These are not personal failures,  
> but structural mismatches.
